Yu-Cheng Chou

CS Ph.D. Student @ Johns Hopkins University, advised by Prof. Alan Yuille
PhD Research Intern @ NVIDIA, advised by Dr. Zhiding Yu

I received my MSE from Johns Hopkins University in 2024, where I was advised by Prof. Zongwei Zhou, and my BSE from Wuhan University in 2022, where I was advised by Prof. Deng-Ping Fan. Previously, I've worked as a research intern at Philips Research, Microsoft Research Asia, Tencent Wesure, and Kuaishou.

I study intelligence in the physical world—building systems that can perceive, reason about, and act within 3D environments. My current work focuses on world models and embodied AI, aiming to bridge visual understanding with real-world interaction. Previously, I worked on scalable training, dataset curation, and annotation for medical imaging.
